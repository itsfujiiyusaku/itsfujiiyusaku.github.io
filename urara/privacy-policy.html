Privacy Policy for Urara

Last updated: October 19, 2025

Overview

urara is a route tracking app that creates 3D flyover animations of your journeys, all processed completely locally on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All route tracking, 3D animation rendering, and screen recording happens entirely on your device. Your data never leaves your device.

Location Data

While the app accesses your device's location services to track routes, this data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your location data.

Screen Recording

Urara uses screen recording to capture your 3D flyover animations for sharing. These recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your recordings.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All your routes, animations, and recordings are stored locally on your device. You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time through the app or by removing Urara from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date.
