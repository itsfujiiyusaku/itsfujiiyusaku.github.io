Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a route?

Track your movement in real-time, and Urara will automatically record your route with location data.

How do I create a 3D flyover animation?

After recording a route, select it and tap the animation button. Urara will render a 3D flyover animation of your journey using native graphics.

Where is my data stored?

All your routes, animations, and recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to any of your data.
